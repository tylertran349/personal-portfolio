In middle school, I took classes at my local library that covered the basics of languages like Python, Java, and HTML. Then, in August 2022, I started doing The Odin Project, a self-guided full-stack web development curriculum, in which I learned various full-stack web development languages, tools, and concepts and created 15+ projects. I love full-stack web development because it allows me to use my artistic ability, creativity, and problem-solving ability to make real-world websites and apps.

In my free time, I like to keep up with all things NFL, listen to NFL-related podcasts, learn about geography, travel to new places, do go-karting, and play Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020.
